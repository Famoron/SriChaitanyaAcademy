About us

Welcome to Sri Chaitanya Academy

Choosing the right path in education along with a befitting educational institute plays an important role in shaping the career of a budding student. Everyone must take a right sion with right choice. Sri Chaitanya Academy provides the perfect platform for the student to fight out his dream career. It shapes the professionality and personality of each student by providing the right type of training.

Sri Chaitanya Academy, Bhubaneswar is in operation since 2012 and is constantly endeavouring to achieve Excellency.

01

Why Choose us

Sri Chaitanya Academy fuels the desire to inculcate a lifelong habit of learning in students, imperative for holistic development. To fuel the seed of learning, the academy constantly motivates the students to believe in the motto “Learning the treasure within.”

02

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform to every students to explore his/her potential, imparting modernised education with eastern values, to create global leaders in every domain, to be a pioneer in quality education and to mould every child into a good human.

03

Our visions

Our vision is to empower students, faculty and employees with values that contribute to universal brotherhood, create great leaders to meet challenges and make a positive contribution to society.

From Director's Desk


Education at Post Secondary stage is a crucial phase in student’s life. This phase sets the tone for an all round personality development including a planned career growth at the college and university level of higher education. We at Sri Chaitanya Academy, Bhubaneswar try to do our best to prepare the students for C.B.S.E / C.H.S.E curriculum based higher secondary education along with for successful career through various national level entrance examinations for entry into the exciting world of engineering, medicine and others. Sri Chaitanya Academy has been going ahead with the above motto for the last seven years. Each student is important for us and we take care for every student for his/her all round development and successful career.
